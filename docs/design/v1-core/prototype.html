Ingredients

1 tspolive oil

100greduced-fat feta
2 tbsp0% Greek yoghurt
Method

Butterfly the chicken and marinate in lemon, olive oil, oregano and garlic, 10 minutes.

Griddle 5–6 minutes each side until charred and cooked through.

Whip the feta with Greek yoghurt and a little lemon until smooth and spreadable.

Toast the buns, spread the whipped feta, and build with chicken, tomato and red onion.

Romy's note · Add a spoon of harissa to the feta if you want a bit of heat.
